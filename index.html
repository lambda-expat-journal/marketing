Expat Journal

An online scrapbook to help you keep track of all your favorite vacations

Expat Journal provides a space for you to upload trip photos and add all the details to help you capture the memories. Keep them private or show them off!

Record Your Memories

How the App Works

  1. Make a new profile
  2. Add a new entry
  3. Upload your picture
  4. Enter the trip information
  5. Enjoy reminiscing about your trip
  6. Share with friends and family!

Features

Lasting Memory Book

You can visit your site to see your trip laid out in a grid

Customizable

You can create, read, update and delete stories and photos
